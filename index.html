About Me

Hello, I’m Gui, Your Innovation Catalyst and Creative Dynamo

With a passion for sparking positive change and a knack for turning complex challenges into opportunities, I’m an Innovation Consultant and Facilitator who thrives at the intersection of creativity, education, and social impact. My journey has always been fueled by a deep curiosity about how creativity works—from the latest discoveries in neuroscience to the rich tapestry of cultural perspectives. Whether it's guiding organizations through transformative projects or crafting workshops that ignite fresh thinking, I’m here to help ideas flourish and create lasting impact. My background in education and creative studies has armed me with the tools to not just think outside the box, but to redesign the box entirely. I believe that creativity is a powerful force—one that can drive meaningful change in the world when harnessed with intention and purpose. That’s why I’m passionate about working on projects that don’t just talk about innovation, but actually live and breathe it, bringing tangible benefits to communities and the environment. And while I take my work seriously, I believe in keeping things light-hearted—because what’s the point of solving the world’s problems if we can’t have a bit of fun along the way? Whether we’re brainstorming the next big idea or facilitating a workshop, expect a dose of humor, a dash of inspiration, and a whole lot of creative energy. Let’s connect and make some magic happen!
